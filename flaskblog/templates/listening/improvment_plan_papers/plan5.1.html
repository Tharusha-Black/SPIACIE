Listening

This type of exercises aims at improving your listening skills for short-answer questions. It requires you to be patient and attentive in order to answer all the questions correctly.

Note that:

  • On the real test, you will hear the recording only once.
  • Short-answer questions are very common on IELTS Listening.

Listen to the recording and try to answer all the given questions:


“Hair of the dog”

Answer the questions below.

Write ONLY ONE WORD from the dialogue for each answer.


  1. Who is Fido?

  2. A bottle of what did Finn buy?

  3. What should the hair of the dog cure?

  4. Hair of the dog was believed to be a remedy for what disease, according to the medieval belief?

  5. Who's going to drink just coffee today?

ANSWERS

Each question correctly answered scores 1 mark. Correct spelling is needed in all answers.

  1. dog
  2. whisky
  3. hangover
  4. rabies
  5. rob
